1. User & Membership

Central system core. Users are categorized by Membership Levels (Intern to Rank 10), determining earning potential and rights.

2. Financial Ecosystem

  • Wallets: Income, Personal, and Tasks.
  • Flows: Deposits and Withdrawals.
  • Incentives: Commissions and Salaries.

3. Rank Upgrades

Tracked via RankUpgradeRequest history, whether via direct deposit or Personal Wallet funds.

4. Earning Activities

Comprises Daily Tasks (watched videos), Wealth Funds (fixed-term investments), and Games (spin results).
